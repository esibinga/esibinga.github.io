My name is Eva Sibinga. I've spent most of my life in and around New York City and in Midcoast Maine.

I'm interested in how digital technologies reproduce and change the analog world, particularly when it comes to race and gender. I'm passionate about bringing together data science and humanistic thought to cultivate a data-driven, digital future that works better for everyone. I believe in the power of data visualization to tell stories that go beyond words and empower people to engage with some realities of our data-drenched world.

When I'm not at my computer, I like birdwatching and playing my violin with other people.
